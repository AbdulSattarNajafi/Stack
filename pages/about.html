About Stack Butler

Ready to serve

Stackbutler is an independent platform that provides information about software products. By analyzing various data points on the internet, we try to paint the best possible picture of a product.

These data points contain: reviews from different comparison platforms, scores from different comparison platforms and a sentiment analysis taken from Google, Twitter and Facebook. We summarize all these scores in a Butler score. The goal of Stackbutler is to give the best possible picture of a product at a glance.

In addition, we provide information about product information, pricing, features, reviews, alternatives and comparisons with other software. Stackbutler is the place to find your new software tools.
